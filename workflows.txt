0. User Authentication Workflow
Users are required to log in before accessing any workflow in the system. Authentication is handled as a common workflow and is assumed to be completed before initiating any of the workflows listed below.

1. Table Reservation and Pre-Order Workflow
Customers can view available tables and seats in real time, reserve a table for a specific time slot, and optionally place a pre-order so that the food is prepared to be ready at the reservation time.



2. Online Ordering Workflow (Delivery and Takeaway)
Customers can browse the menu, place orders for delivery or takeaway, make payments, and track the status of their orders.
Workflow Specification:
This workflow describes how customers place online food orders for delivery or takeaway through the system.

Step-by-Step Workflow Description:
1. The customer opens the online ordering section of the application.
2. The system displays the restaurant menu with item details and prices.
3. The customer selects menu items and adds them to the cart.
4. The customer chooses the order type (delivery or takeaway).
5. The customer enters delivery details if delivery is selected.
6. The customer proceeds to the payment process.
7. The system confirms the payment and places the order.
8. The order is sent to the restaurant for preparation.
9. The system updates the order status during preparation and fulfillment.
10. The customer tracks the order until delivery or pickup.

Key Features and Functionalities:
- Online menu browsing
- Cart and order management
- Delivery and takeaway options
- Online payment processing
- Order status tracking



3. Catering and Event Booking Workflow
Customers can request catering services or book the restaurant for events by specifying event details such as date, time, number of guests, and menu preferences, which are then reviewed and confirmed by the restaurant.
